Transfer money

As an association board member you can transfer money to user accounts or other associations. Use this to allow members of any association to deposit money in their account. The procedure for this is:

  • An association member can ask you to deposit money in their account.
  • You receive the money and store it in the cash box of your association (if it's cash), or hand it over to the treasurer.
  • You transfer the money to their account using this page.

The money remains theirs which is why the association balance decreases by the transfer amount! At any moment your association can repay the money received from users to Scala to correct the negative balance of your association. Whenever the user pays for kitchen cost, the amount is transferred to a special kitchen cost account, Scala uses this account for the kitchen payment.

A money transfer can only be undone by the receiving party! Check the fields carefully before creating a new transaction.
