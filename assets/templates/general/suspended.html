Causes for suspended accounts

There are generally two reasons why accounts are suspended:

  1. An account has been determined not to be a member of the Scala associations. Either because that user was never a member or ceized to be a member. This is detected automatically by the system.
  2. The user broke the rules in the agreement and/or has done actions related to the use of the app or the kitchen that resulted in a suspension.

What has been disabled

Suspended accounts can no longer:

  1. Create dining lists
  2. Add others to dining lists.
  3. Post comments to dining lists.
  4. Transfer money to other members.
  5. Adjust their allergies
Suspended accounts can:
  1. Join dining lists as longer as their balance remains positive
  2. Can be added by others as long as their balance remains positive
  3. An exception above is made for members of an association with no negative restrictions.

How to fix it

Depending on the reason of suspension you can reactivate your account as follows:

If your account was suspended due to no longer being a member, your account is re-activated as soon as your association reconfirms your membership. In your settings, mark what associations you are part of and wait for those associations to confirm that. If an association is incorrectly marked as rejected, please contact the board of that association.

If your account has suspended because of another reason, you are likely to have recieved how long your account will be suspended.
If you do not agree with this suspension, contact the Scala board (bestuur@studentencultuur.nl) or the dining app admins (diningapp@studentencultuur.nl)
